### **Interview‑Style Answer: What is Kubernetes?**

Kubernetes is an **open‑source container orchestration platform** used to **deploy, manage, scale, and operate containerized applications**. It automates tasks such as container scheduling, service discovery, load balancing, scaling, and self‑healing, making it easier to run applications reliably in production.

In simple terms, Kubernetes acts as a **control system** that ensures containers are running where they should be, in the desired state, and are automatically restarted, replaced, or scaled when needed.

### Key features of Kubernetes:

*   **Automatic scaling** – scales applications up or down based on demand
*   **Self‑healing** – restarts failed containers and replaces unhealthy pods
*   **Load balancing & service discovery** – distributes traffic across containers
*   **Rolling updates & rollbacks** – deploys changes without downtime
*   **Configuration & secret management** – manages app configurations securely

> *Kubernetes is a platform that automates the deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications.*
